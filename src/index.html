About Mines of Jupiter

Mines of Jupiter is more than just an artist's residency—it's a private sanctuary for deep focus and creative discovery, located on a quiet working farm on the island of Møn.

The Studio

Step into a modern, light-filled studio with high ceilings and an east-facing skylight. Enjoy a private bedroom, kitchenette, bathroom, and loft space. Wake up to the sunrise over ancient burial mounds—an inspiring view that connects you to history and nature.

The Location

Nestled on the west side of Møn, the studio is close to beaches, historic churches, and the harbor. The island is known for its unique Ice Age landscapes and thriving art scene, making it a haven for creativity and reflection.

The Experience

Share the farm with a few chickens and a friendly cat, and recharge with optional sessions in the wood-heated sauna and cold tub. Whether you're a writer, musician, or visual artist, this is a place designed for focus, flow, and inspiration.

Celestial Inspiration

The name “Mines of Jupiter” is inspired by an old Jimi Hendrix song—setting the tone for a stay that is creative, cosmic, and unforgettable.
